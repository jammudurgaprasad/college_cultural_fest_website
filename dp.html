Instructions for Fine Arts

  1. Ensure all materials (paints, brushes, canvases) are available and ready before the event.
  2. Participants should arrive at least 30 minutes prior to the start for registration and setup.
  3. Artwork must adhere to the given theme; off-theme submissions may be disqualified.
  4. All participants must clean their work area after completion.
  5. Judges' decisions will be final, and participants must respect the evaluation process.
  6. Age must be between 18 and 30 years.
  7. Double-check your information before submitting the form.
